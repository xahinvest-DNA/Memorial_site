# Tasks

## Active phase: Discovery

### D-001 — Manufacturer discovery package

Status: READY FOR OWNER INPUT

Collect catalogue, price rules, production stages, capabilities, lead times, warranty rules, sample policy and representative completed orders from the known manufacturer.

### D-002 — Sales and customer journey evidence

Status: PLANNED

Collect real customer questions, objections, estimate examples, contract stages, payment patterns, reasons for delay and reasons for refusal.

### D-003 — Installation service blueprint

Status: PLANNED

Map site survey, cemetery checks, foundation choice, delivery, unloading, installation, photo evidence, acceptance and warranty.

### D-004 — Buyer interviews

Status: PLANNED

Interview recent buyers or family decision-makers and convert findings into jobs, anxieties, information needs and failure modes.

### D-005 — Pricing confidence model

Status: BLOCKED BY D-001/D-003

Define which prices can be exact, ranged, conditional or available only after specialist review.

### D-006 — Validated service blueprint

Status: BLOCKED BY D-001–D-004

Create a shared map across customer, manager, designer, manufacturer, supplier and installer.

### D-007 — MVP boundary and metrics

Status: BLOCKED BY D-005/D-006

Choose the first complete vertical journey, explicit exclusions and measurable success criteria.

### D-008 — Product Gate review

Status: BLOCKED

Review evidence, risks, service blueprint, revised product specification and Release 1 roadmap. Product Owner decides whether implementation may begin.

## Task state definitions

- `PLANNED` — identified but not ready.
- `READY FOR OWNER INPUT` — requires business evidence from Product Owner.
- `READY FOR MANAGER` — enough input exists for manager analysis.
- `READY FOR CODEX` — approved Issue exists and dependencies are resolved.
- `IN PROGRESS` — active branch/PR exists.
- `IN REVIEW` — manager is reviewing evidence.
- `DONE` — merged and reflected in Current State.
- `BLOCKED` — dependency or decision prevents progress.
