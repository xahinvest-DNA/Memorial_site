## Current product hypothesis

A conventional catalogue website is insufficient. The product should be a vertically integrated purchase infrastructure connecting:

1. orientation and guided choice;
2. memorial brief and specification;
3. transparent proposal and versions;
4. collaborative approvals;
5. contract and payments;
6. production, delivery and installation;
7. evidence, acceptance and warranty.
